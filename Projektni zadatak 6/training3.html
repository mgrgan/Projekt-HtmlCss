Group training

Good company, excellent music, a large number of different programs, an excellent trainer and the pleasant atmosphere of the hall is one of the important reasons why we are visited so much.

Define your body, get rid of stress, regulate your weight, gain weight and the show can begin. Join the group exercises of our gym.

We are offering many group programs like pilates, yoga, group cardio sessions and calisthenics

Group personal training offers valued training at a fraction of the price of an individual trainer. This appeals to members who cannot routinely afford a trainer in a one-on-one setting. Specialized training in a group setting creates a social motivation to make lifestyle changes. Those who generally require more guidance can be found in group fitness (larger groups of 50 to 60 people) benefit from specialized training. Additionally, a group training program can attract new prospective training partners who are willing to motivate each other.
